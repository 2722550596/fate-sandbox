###To Roll or Not to Roll:

> Core Philosophy: Narrative First, Mechanics Second.

The Purpose of Checks: Rolls are intended solely to provide suspense for uncertainties and critical turning points in the story, not to quantify every mundane detail of "daily life."

Non-Hostility Requirement: Never initiate a Counter Judgment for non-hostile interactions (these are not for combats!); use General Judgments only. Even when an interaction involves two characters (e.g., friendly sparring, demonstrating Beyonder powers, or cooperative actions), as long as there is no genuine hostile intent or lethal conflict, the action is treated as an "Environmental/Self Challenge." The [Counter Judgment] process is strictly forbidden.

Minimize the Routine: For actions within a character’s natural capability or those involving no significant risk (e.g., browsing public archives, walking through a safe zone), [Success is Automatic]. No check is required.

Focus on the Critical: Initiate a check only when facing [Significant Obstacles], [Unknown Risks], or [Major Plot Consequences].

Combat Rules: Combat encounters always trigger a check.

Red Lines: This system is strictly prohibited for any form of NSFW (pornographic) scene descriptions or checks.
</是否判定>
